Content Creators

I am creating a website that makes it easy for you, a content creator to organize your social media posts. This is meant to help you make sure you share on all platforms. There will be a simple process: 1. Where are you sharing content? 2. Selfless Promotion.

Process

  1. Will you be streaming live on twitch, posting a video on youtube, or sharing a photo on instagram?
  2. Promote your content by sharing a post on all social media platforms.

Notes

  • Provide a link to your content in your promotional post.
  • Connect your various social medias through the same account for easier sharing.
  • Set a schedule for when you stream and post videos.
  • Instagram can be used for promotion and can be promoted.
  • Get some fresh air, take a break away from the screen.

Double Check

  • There is a link to other social media accounts on every site for easy access.
  • Give credit where credit is due.
  • Promote your guests social media if available.
  • Did you upload on schedule? do you need to re-schedule?
